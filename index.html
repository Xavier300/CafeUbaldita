About Cafe Ubaldita

Founded in 2023, Cafe Ubaldita is a restaurant with a focus on healthful milk tea. The idea was primarily sparked by the many cultures around sipping tea, which is a significant aspect of customer entertainment rituals in many Asian nations and shows respect and appreciation for the visitors.

Guaranteed to be created from 100% freshly brewed, premium loose-leaf teas, Cafe Ubaldita beverages are interesting due to their many flavors and blends, which contribute to a joyful and healthy lifestyle.

Our Goal

Our primary motivation for starting the business was to instill in Filipinos the importance of providing quality tea for people who seek to appreciate the beauty of life.
